About Me

I'm Troy Lussier, a passionate developer with a unique journey into the world of technology. After dedicating over 20 years to carpentry, building everything from custom homes to intricate woodwork, a workplace injury redirected my path toward coding and web development.

What started as a necessity became a passion. The problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and craftsmanship mindset I developed as a carpenter translate perfectly to writing clean, efficient code. I approach each project with the same dedication to quality and precision that I brought to every construction job.

My transition from building physical structures to creating digital experiences has been incredibly rewarding. I continuously learn and adapt to new technologies, bringing both the work ethic of a seasoned tradesman and the fresh perspective of someone who chose coding as their second career.
